Interaction Guide
  • Flip pages: click on a page side to turn forward or backward.
  • Move view: hold Space and drag with left click.
  • Zoom: use the slider, + / - buttons, or keyboard + / -.
  • Reset view: Reset returns zoom and position only (does not flip pages).
  • Quick navigation: Arrow Right for next spread, Arrow Left for previous spread.
